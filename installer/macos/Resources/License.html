Anti-abuse: all traffic relayed through your machine passes a CSAM-hash + PhishTank pre-filter before leaving your network. You can audit every byte from your dashboard. We do not, and cannot, decrypt customer HTTPS payloads.

Privacy: iogrid does not collect personal data about your computer or its other applications. Only the daemon's own metrics (cycles contributed, bandwidth used, workload categories accepted) are reported.
