Welcome to iogrid

This installer will:

  • Install the iogrid daemon to /usr/local/iogrid
  • Register a LaunchAgent in your account so it runs on login
  • Open your browser to finish sign-in and pick your sharing defaults

The daemon runs as your user, not root. It will only contribute idle cycles when your machine isn't in use, with a 50 GB/month bandwidth cap by default.

You can change every setting (or stop sharing entirely) from your iogrid dashboard at any time.
