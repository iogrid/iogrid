## Deflationary mechanism — three layers

### Layer 1 — Buyback-and-burn from customer revenue

- Customers pay in USD (Stripe), USDC (on-chain), or $GRID directly.
- A daily automated process converts **2% of all revenue** to $GRID via
  Jupiter swap, then sends to the well-known Solana burn address.
- Burns are public, on-chain, verifiable at `burn.iogrid.org`.

### Layer 2 — Emission halving (every 24 months)

| Year | Provider emission rate |
|---|---|
| 0–2 | 50M / year |
| 2–4 | 25M / year |
| 4–6 | 12.5M / year |
| 6–8 | 6.25M / year |
| 8–10 | 3.125M / year |
| 10+ | 0 new emissions; only burns reduce supply |

Hard-coded into the SPL emission program; no governance override.

### Layer 3 — Mandatory provider-earnings lockup

**Every $GRID earned by a provider is auto-locked the moment it's
distributed.** Providers cannot sell freshly earned tokens.

Base schedule applied to ALL earned $GRID:

| Time since earned | % unlocked |
|---|---|
| Day 0 – 30 | 0% (cliff) |
| Day 30 – 90 | Linear vest 0% → 100% |
| Day 90+ | 100% unlocked |

Rolling per payout — every weekly distribution starts its own 30/90-day clock.

### Why it works

1. **Stops day-1 dump.** Without lockup, every provider would convert
   $GRID → USDC on receipt. With lockup, ~33% of any month's earnings are
   sellable at any moment.
2. **Compounds with deflation.** Locked tokens count toward "circulating
   supply removed".
3. **Skin in the game.** Providers care about $GRID price even after they
   stop providing.

### Optional bonus lockup tiers

Providers can elect a **longer** lockup at signup for a rewards multiplier:

| Lockup tier | Cliff + vest schedule | Multiplier |
|---|---|---|
| **Standard** (default) | 30-day cliff + 60-day vest | 1.0× |
| **Loyalty** | 90-day cliff + 180-day vest | 1.25× |
| **Conviction** | 180-day cliff + 365-day vest | 1.5× |
| **Maximum** | 365-day cliff + 730-day vest | 2.0× |

Tier can be upgraded any time, never downgraded.

### Early-unlock escape hatch

A provider in genuine financial need can early-unlock locked $GRID — with a
**50% penalty**, which is burned (strengthens deflation). One per provider
per 12 months (anti-gaming).

## What $GRID is **not**

- **Not a security** — utility token only. No claim on iogrid revenue, no
  voting rights over corporate decisions, no equity equivalence.
- **Not a stablecoin** — value is market-determined, no peg.
- **Not a payment processor replacement** — customers can pay in fiat
  (Stripe) or USDC; $GRID is one option among three.
- **Not a price prediction tool** — see disclaimer below.
