iogrid bills monthly. Every plan includes the platform fee plus
usage-metered charges per workload. There are no per-seat fees, no
per-API-call surcharges, no overage penalties beyond the metered rate.

iOS-build pricing is flat across plans because that's the wedge we want to
preserve — we'd rather have many small customers than gate the savings behind
an enterprise tier.

## Billing cycle

- Invoices issued on the 1st of each month for the previous month's usage.
- Net-30 payment terms; auto-debit via Stripe by default.
- Outstanding invoices over 30 days disable new workload submissions
  (returns `INVOICE_OVERDUE`) but keep your API key alive for read-only ops.

## Annual commits

Business and Enterprise plans support annual prepayment with 10% discount
($199 × 12 × 0.9 = $2148 → $1933 annually). Usage is metered against the
balance month-over-month.

## Stripe customer portal

Manage payment methods, download invoices, update billing email at
[dashboard.iogrid.org/billing/portal](https://dashboard.iogrid.org/billing/portal).
The portal is hosted by Stripe — iogrid never touches your card data.

## Quotas

- Maximum concurrent workloads (above)
- Maximum spend per day (defaults to $100 USD-equivalent; raisable in dashboard)
- Maximum API keys per account (10 on Free / Pro, unlimited on Business+)

## Refunds

iogrid issues refunds for **service incidents** under the SLA. We do not
issue refunds for:

- Customer-side bugs or misconfiguration
- Workload failures caused by provider mid-run dropouts that the scheduler
  retried successfully (only the failed first attempt is charged; retries are
  free)
- Spending past your daily cap (set the cap appropriately)
