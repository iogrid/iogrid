Providers choose one of four payout currencies. All four can be changed any
time; tier choice affects future earnings only — never retroactive.

## The four tiers

## Setting and switching tiers

In the daemon UI at `localhost:7777` → **Payouts**, or in the hosted
dashboard at `dashboard.iogrid.org/provide/payouts`. The tier you select now
applies to all future earnings until you change it again.

## Tier 1 — Cash mechanics

1. Provider links a Stripe Connect Express account (US, EU, UK, Canada, AU
   supported at launch; more countries quarterly).
2. Earnings accumulate in your iogrid balance after a 7-day clearing period
   (matches Stripe's default settlement).
3. Once balance ≥ $10, you can trigger payout from the dashboard. Auto-payout
   on a monthly schedule is the default.
4. **1099-NEC** filed at year-end for US providers earning over $600.
5. **VAT** handling: EU providers with valid VAT numbers can mark their
   account VAT-registered; iogrid issues VAT-compliant payout statements.

## Tier 2 — Free VPN mechanics

1. Provider enables Tier 2 in the dashboard.
2. The iogrid VPN apps for iOS / Android / Mac / Windows / Linux become
   unlimited for that account. Up to 5 devices per provider account.
3. Cash earnings are diverted to the VPN-credit pool (1:1 conversion: $0.20
   of bandwidth-share buys $0.20 of VPN compute, paying for a full month).
4. **If a provider earns more than $0.20/month**, the surplus rolls over as
   a VPN credit for future months. After 12 months of accumulated credit,
   the surplus can convert back to cash (Tier 1) on request.

## Tier 3 — $GRID mechanics

1. Provider enables Tier 3 and provides a Solana wallet address (we
   recommend Phantom or Solflare).
2. Bandwidth + workload earnings convert to $GRID at the daily TWAP rate.
3. $GRID is **auto-locked** for the elected lockup tier:
   - Standard (default): 30-day cliff + 60-day linear vest, 1.0× multiplier
   - Loyalty: 90-day cliff + 180-day vest, 1.25× multiplier
   - Conviction: 180-day cliff + 365-day vest, 1.5× multiplier
   - Maximum: 365-day cliff + 730-day vest, 2.0× multiplier
4. Once unlocked, $GRID transfers to the provider's wallet on a weekly
   schedule. Provider can then HODL, transfer, sell on Raydium / Jupiter, etc.
5. **Early-unlock** is available with a 50% penalty (burned). One per
   12 months per provider.
6. **Tax treatment** varies by jurisdiction; consult a qualified advisor.
   iogrid issues a year-end statement of $GRID earnings denominated in USD at
   the time-of-earn TWAP.

<Aside type="caution" title="Crypto-asset risks">
  $GRID's value is market-determined and may go down. Providers electing
  Tier 3 are betting on iogrid's growth thesis. Read the
  [tokenomics page](/concepts/tokenomics/) before opting in.
</Aside>

## Tier 4 — Charity mechanics

1. Provider selects a charity from the iogrid charity registry.
2. Bandwidth + workload earnings accumulate as in Tier 1.
3. iogrid issues a quarterly bulk donation to the charity, attributing each
   provider's share. Charity issues a tax receipt directly to the provider.
4. iogrid takes 0% admin fee on charity payouts.

Current charity registry:

- **Electronic Frontier Foundation** (defending digital rights, since 1990)
- **Tor Project** (free, open-source anonymity software)
- **Wikipedia / Wikimedia Foundation** (free knowledge)
- **Doctors Without Borders / MSF** (medical aid in crisis zones)
- **Internet Archive** (preserving the web for posterity)
- **Khan Academy** (free education globally)

Want a charity added? Email
[charities@iogrid.org](mailto:charities@iogrid.org); we vet for legitimacy
(IRS 501c3 / EU equivalent) and add quarterly.

## Switching between tiers

- Switching is **instant**. The new tier applies to the next earnings tick.
- Already-locked $GRID stays locked (Tier 3 commitments don't unwind).
- Already-accrued cash balance can be paid out or converted on request.
- Charity donations already accrued are forwarded on the next quarterly tick.
