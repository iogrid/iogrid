## Three audiences, three relationships

iogrid is processing data on behalf of three distinct groups:

- **Customers** — companies using iogrid's API to do work
- **Providers** — individuals sharing their device's idle capacity
- **End users** — your customers, who may interact with your services that
  run on iogrid

This Privacy Policy describes our practices for all three.

## What we collect — customers

- Account info: email, name, billing address, payment method (Stripe-tokenized)
- API usage telemetry: request counts, workload submissions, billable units
- Workload metadata: image refs, run specs, secrets (KMS-encrypted)
- Support communications

## What we collect — providers

- Account info: email, country, payout currency, Stripe Connect ID
- Device telemetry: CPU/RAM utilization, uptime, OS version, GPU model
- Bandwidth + workload metadata: destination categories, byte counts (audit log)
- $GRID wallet address if Tier 3 payout chosen

## What we collect — end users

We **do not** collect identifying data about your customers via the
bandwidth workload. Customer HTTPS traffic is end-to-end encrypted; iogrid
sees only the **destination** and the encrypted byte stream, not the content.

For Docker / GPU workloads, end-user data is whatever your container handles
— iogrid is a processor, not a controller, of that data.

## Legal bases (GDPR)

| Purpose | Lawful basis |
|---|---|
| Service provision | Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| Billing | Contract |
| Anti-abuse | Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) |
| Marketing emails (opt-in only) | Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) |
| Law-enforcement cooperation | Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)) |

## Data retention

| Data | Retention |
|---|---|
| Customer account info | While account active + 30 days after closure |
| Billing data | 7 years (regulatory) |
| API usage logs | 90 days |
| Workload images / specs | 30 days post-completion |
| Workload secrets | Until customer deletes |
| Provider audit log | 90 days |
| Provider account info | While account active + 30 days |

## Your rights

You can:

- **Access** your data
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your account and data (subject to billing-record retention)
- **Export** your data in machine-readable form
- **Object** to specific processing
- **Lodge a complaint** with your data protection authority

Email [privacy@iogrid.org](mailto:privacy@iogrid.org) to exercise any of
these. We respond within 30 days (sooner if straightforward).

## International transfers

iogrid is based in the EU. Customers worldwide may use the service. Data
transferred outside the EEA flows under SCCs (Standard Contractual Clauses)
where applicable.

## Cookies

The marketing site uses essential cookies only. The dashboard uses session
cookies (httpOnly, secure, SameSite=Lax) plus optional analytics cookies
(opt-in via consent banner). No third-party advertising or cross-site
tracking cookies.
