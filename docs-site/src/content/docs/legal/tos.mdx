## What this page is

iogrid's customer Terms of Service in plain English. The actual binding text
lives on the marketing site at the URL above — it's drafted by qualified
counsel and is the authoritative source. This documentation page is here so
developers can quickly understand the legal posture without first navigating
out of the docs.

## High-level summary

By using the iogrid API or dashboard, you agree to:

1. Use the service in accordance with all applicable laws and the
   [Acceptable Use Policy](/legal/aup/).
2. Be responsible for **the content of any traffic** you initiate. iogrid is
   the platform; the customer is the actor.
3. Pay the metered fees per [billing/plans](/billing/plans/).
4. Hold iogrid harmless from claims arising from your use of the service
   (indemnity clause).
5. Comply with iogrid's anti-abuse rules — see [security/anti-abuse](/security/anti-abuse/).

iogrid agrees to:

1. Provide the service per the [SLA tier you've purchased](/billing/plans/#plan-matrix).
2. Keep your data private per the [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy/).
3. Notify you of any law-enforcement request that targets your account (unless gagged).
4. Refund usage during qualifying SLA incidents.
